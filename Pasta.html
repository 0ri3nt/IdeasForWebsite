How to make Pastaüçù

1. Put the pasta in a large bowl.
2. Pour water so that it covers all of the pasta.
3. Heat until boil.
4. Start the preparation of the sauce.
5. After the pasta is boiled, strain the water.
6. Add the pasta to the sauce you have prepared.
7. Mix it up well and add cheese and seasoning.
8. Serve with garlic bread.
9. Enjoy!
